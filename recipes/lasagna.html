Lasagne

A classic Italian dish with layers of meat sauce, noodles, and cheese, baked to perfection and served piping hot. This recipe guides you through creating a flavorful meat sauce, preparing the ricotta mixture, layering the lasagna, and achieving a deliciously cheesy and hearty result.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Egg
  • Parsley
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain the excess fat.
  3. Add onions and garlic to the skillet and sauté until they are softened.
  4. Pour in the tomato sauce, crushed tomatoes, and tomato paste. Stir well and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a separate bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, parsley, salt, and pepper. Mix until well blended.
  6. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  7. Place a layer of lasagna noodles on top of the sauce, slightly overlapping them.
  8. Spread a layer of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  9. Add a layer of shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese.
  10. Repeat the layers: meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and cheese.
  11. Finish with a final layer of noodles, meat sauce, and a generous amount of cheese on top.
  12. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes.
  13. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  14. Allow the lasagna to cool for 10-15 minutes before serving to set and enhance the flavors.
  15. Serve hot and enjoy!
