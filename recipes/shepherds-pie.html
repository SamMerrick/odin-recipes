Shepherd's Pie

A satisfying and comforting dish that combines savory ground meat, mixed vegetables, and creamy mashed potatoes. This recipe guides you through browning the meat, adding flavorful seasonings, assembling the layers of meat and vegetables, topping it off with a creamy layer of mashed potatoes, and baking it to golden perfection. A hearty and delicious meal for the whole family to enjoy.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ground lamb or beef
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Dried thyme
  • Dried rosemary
  • Frozen mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn)
  • Potatoes
  • Butter
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add diced onions and minced garlic to the skillet. Sauté until the onions are translucent and the garlic is fragrant.
  4. Add ground lamb or beef to the skillet and cook until browned.
  5. Season the meat with salt, pepper, Worcestershire sauce, and dried herbs like thyme and rosemary. Stir well to combine.
  6. Add frozen mixed vegetables (such as peas, carrots, and corn) to the skillet. Cook for a few minutes until the vegetables are thawed and heated through.
  7. In a separate pot, prepare mashed potatoes by boiling peeled and cubed potatoes until tender.
  8. Drain the potatoes and return them to the pot.
  9. Add butter, milk, salt, and pepper to the potatoes. Mash until smooth and creamy.
  10. Transfer the meat and vegetable mixture to a baking dish.
  11. Spread the mashed potatoes evenly over the meat mixture, creating a top layer.
  12. Use a fork to create decorative ridges on the surface of the mashed potatoes.
  13. Bake the Shepherd's Pie in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the top is golden and the filling is bubbling.
  14. Remove the dish from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
  15. Serve the Shepherd's Pie hot and enjoy!
