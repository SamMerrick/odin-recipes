Chicken Soup

A comforting and nourishing soup made with tender chicken, vegetables, and aromatic seasonings. This recipe takes you through the steps of sautéing the vegetables, cooking the chicken in flavorful broth, adding the noodles, and finishing it off with a sprinkle of fresh herbs. Perfect for warming up on a chilly day or when you need a comforting bowl of goodness.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Onion
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Garlic
  • Boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Chicken broth
  • Dried thyme
  • Dried rosemary
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Noodles (such as egg noodles or rotini)
  • Fresh parsley

Steps

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add diced onions, carrots, and celery to the pot. Sauté until the vegetables are softened.
  3. Add minced garlic and sauté for an additional minute until fragrant.
  4. Add boneless, skinless chicken breasts to the pot and cook until they are lightly browned on the outside.
  5. Pour in chicken broth and bring the mixture to a boil.
  6. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for about 20 minutes until the chicken is cooked through.
  7. Remove the chicken from the pot and shred it into bite-sized pieces using two forks.
  8. Return the shredded chicken to the pot.
  9. Add dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and black pepper to the soup. Stir to combine.
  10. Add noodles of your choice (such as egg noodles or rotini) and cook according to the package instructions.
  11. Once the noodles are cooked, taste the soup and adjust the seasoning if needed.
  12. Stir in fresh chopped parsley for added freshness.
  13. Remove the pot from the heat and let the soup rest for a few minutes before serving.
  14. Ladle the hot chicken soup into bowls and serve it with crusty bread or crackers.
  15. Enjoy your delicious homemade chicken soup!
